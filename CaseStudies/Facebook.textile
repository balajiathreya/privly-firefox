h1. Facebook Case Study

So far Facebook has not put any barriers to replacing the content of an anchor tag with the PrivateWeb content. We could simply replace the content of the tag with our content. We should probably also strip the onmousedown event and any other tracking facilities within the link. We should only leave the href the same.
